A metaphor on build-outs

Landing Page

June 6, 2025

Life is wild. Not really sure what to make of starting over but trying to make the most of my privilege and opportunity. If nothing else, at least I get to practice creating something new and for that I am gracious.

This webpage is nothing right now and maybe it will stay like that for a while. A rock in the corner of my repository. Maybe in 10 years it'll be more laid out. What I want the most is to pay myself dividends and write every day. There are so many thoughts I'd like to share. Most of those thoughts stay imaginary. Making a blog let's me reference past thoughts and ideas that I've shelved. This projects makes me hold myself accountable to make my ideas real.

Going back to school for computer science is scary. Starting over is scary. Feeling like nothing in this system is demoralizing. Since I have nothing else to lose, I should have taken the leap and risk years ago. For myself and not what others think.

I've seen various videos of others taking the leap of faith and figuring it out. Quitting their 9-5 and finding what fulfills them. Like many I want to be fulfilled. So cheers to a starting over, build-outs, and waking up everyday to build something.

p.s. Thank you to the wonderful people of The Odin Project for jump starting this mindset.
